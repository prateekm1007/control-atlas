**Visual Proof of Mechanism**

## 1. Intent
To demonstrate **steric exclusion**. We align the Drug-Bound State (6OIM) with the Effector-Bound State (4G0N). The visualization shows the RAF effector colliding with the drug-locked conformation.

## 2. Technical
*   **Alignment:** `match #2/A to #1/A` (RMSD minimization on G-domain).
*   **Visuals:**
    *   Gray: Drug-Bound KRAS.
    *   Red: AMG 510 Inhibitor.
    *   Tan: RAF-RBD (Phantom position).
    *   Orange: The steric clash zone.

## 3. Interpretation
The drug does not just fill a pocket; it enforces a surface topology that physically rejects the effector.
